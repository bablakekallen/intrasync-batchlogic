Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BatchLogic batch plant control system

What is BatchLogic?

BatchLogic is an advanced batch plant control system designed specifically for concrete producers. It features universal equipment integration that works with any hardware that digitally records data, AI-powered automation, and real-time monitoring to achieve 98% batch accuracy and 30% waste reduction. The system works with all major batching equipment brands.

What makes BatchLogic different from my current controller?

BatchLogic adds precision, automation, and intelligence through software that integrates with your existing equipment. AI algorithms continuously optimize batches, adapt to conditions, and predict maintenance needs. The system works with any brand of equipment that digitally records data, so you keep your investment while gaining modern control and analytics.

How accurate is BatchLogic?

BatchLogic achieves 98% batch accuracy—meaning virtually every batch hits your specifications precisely. This is accomplished through ±0.1% precision load cells, real-time moisture compensation, temperature control, and AI optimization. Plants can expect immediate quality improvements and significant reduction in rework rates.

Will BatchLogic work with my existing equipment?

Yes. BatchLogic integrates with ALL major batch plant equipment—Command Alkon, ConTech, Astec, Liebherr, Hess, Elkon, Olin, and others. We offer three integration approaches: Overlay mode (run alongside your current controller), Full Replacement (complete system upgrade), or Hybrid (mix old and new capabilities). Our team handles all integration planning and execution.

What is the implementation timeline?

Implementation depends on your approach. Overlay mode: 1-2 days (zero production downtime possible). Hybrid mode: 2-3 days. Full replacement: 3-5 days planned downtime. Add 1-2 weeks for initial assessment and configuration. Plants can expect immediate quality improvements and to be fully productive within the first week.

What results should I expect?

Expected results include: 98% batch accuracy, 30% material waste reduction, 15% throughput increase, 45% downtime reduction through predictive maintenance, 89% quality variance reduction, and $150K+ annual savings. BatchLogic is designed to deliver positive ROI within 6 months. Results vary based on current operations and adoption level.

How does the AI automation work?

BatchLogic's AI continuously learns from your plant's batching patterns. Machine learning models optimize water content, temperature, and admixture dispensing based on environmental conditions and aggregate characteristics. The system makes autonomous adjustments during batching to compensate for moisture, temperature, and material variations—producing perfect batches automatically without operator intervention.

What about predictive maintenance?

AI monitors all sensor data and identifies patterns indicating equipment degradation. The system predicts failures weeks in advance with maintenance recommendations—preventing unexpected downtime. This reduces maintenance costs by 25% while extending equipment life. You'll know exactly when to service equipment before problems occur.

Can I control batching remotely?

Yes. BatchLogic includes mobile apps for iOS and Android plus cloud-based dashboards accessible from any device. Plant managers can monitor batching in real-time, view analytics, adjust settings, and receive alerts remotely. The system works offline and automatically syncs when connectivity returns.

How is my data secured?

Enterprise-grade security includes data encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance and conduct annual security audits. Data is hosted on secure AWS servers with automatic backups every 6 hours, 99.5% uptime SLA, and disaster recovery protocols. You own all your data and can export it anytime.

How is BatchLogic priced compared to other batch control systems?

BatchLogic offers service fees that are significantly more affordable than any existing batch control solutions in the market. We provide low onboarding fees that can be spread throughout the term of your contract, making it accessible for plants of all sizes. Pricing is customized based on plant size, number of batching lines, and integration approach. Our service fees include software, implementation, training, and 24/7 support. BatchLogic is designed to deliver ROI within 6 months through waste reduction and efficiency gains, making it cost-positive quickly. Contact us for a personalized quote and ROI analysis.

What about training and support?

Complete training is included for all operators, supervisors, and management across all shifts. We provide 24/7 emergency support for critical issues (response under 15 minutes) plus standard business hours support. Ongoing optimization reviews quarterly. On-site engineers available during implementation and first 30 days of production.

Can I try BatchLogic before purchasing?

We offer personalized demos using your plant data and equipment specifications to show projected results. While we don't offer free trials due to implementation complexity, we provide detailed ROI projections based on your specific operation. Contact us for a personalized assessment.

What equipment is included?

BatchLogic integrates with your existing hardware including load cells, PLCs, moisture sensors, temperature probes, admixture meters, and control systems from any manufacturer. We connect to any equipment that digitally records data, allowing you to keep your current investment while gaining advanced software capabilities.

BatchLogic is software that works with your existing equipment—we don't sell hardware. If you need to upgrade load cells or sensors, we can recommend compatible equipment from trusted manufacturers. Our software integrates with equipment from any vendor, so you have complete flexibility in hardware choices.

How often are updates released?

BatchLogic receives continuous updates with critical improvements deployed weekly and new features released monthly. Major releases happen quarterly.
