Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BatchLogic batch plant control system

What is BatchLogic?

BatchLogic is an advanced batch plant control system designed specifically for concrete producers. It combines proprietary hardware (precision load cells and control units), universal equipment integration, AI-powered automation, and real-time monitoring to achieve 99.9% batch accuracy and 30% waste reduction. The system works with all major batching equipment brands.

What makes BatchLogic different from my current controller?

BatchLogic adds precision, automation, and intelligence. Our proprietary load cells deliver ±0.1% accuracy (10x better than standard equipment). AI algorithms continuously optimize batches, adapt to conditions, and predict maintenance needs. The system works with any brand of equipment, so you keep your investment while gaining modern control and analytics.

How accurate is BatchLogic?

BatchLogic achieves 99.9% batch accuracy—meaning virtually every batch hits your specifications precisely. This is accomplished through ±0.1% precision load cells, real-time moisture compensation, temperature control, and AI optimization. Most plants see immediate quality improvements and significant reduction in rework rates.

Will BatchLogic work with my existing equipment?

Yes. BatchLogic integrates with ALL major batch plant equipment—Command Alkon, ConTech, Astec, Liebherr, Hess, Elkon, Olin, and others. We offer three integration approaches: Overlay mode (run alongside your current controller), Full Replacement (complete system upgrade), or Hybrid (mix old and new capabilities). Our team handles all integration planning and execution.

What is the implementation timeline?

Implementation depends on your approach. Overlay mode: 1-2 days (zero production downtime possible). Hybrid mode: 2-3 days. Full replacement: 3-5 days planned downtime. Add 1-2 weeks for initial assessment and configuration. Most plants see immediate quality improvements and are fully productive within the first week.

What results should I expect?

Typical results include: 99.9% batch accuracy, 30% material waste reduction, 15% throughput increase, 45% downtime reduction through predictive maintenance, 89% quality variance reduction, and $150K+ annual savings. Most plants achieve positive ROI within 6 months. Results vary based on current operations and adoption level.

How does the AI automation work?

BatchLogic's AI continuously learns from your plant's batching patterns. Machine learning models optimize water content, temperature, and admixture dispensing based on environmental conditions and aggregate characteristics. The system makes autonomous adjustments during batching to compensate for moisture, temperature, and material variations—producing perfect batches automatically without operator intervention.

What about predictive maintenance?

AI monitors all sensor data and identifies patterns indicating equipment degradation. The system predicts failures weeks in advance with maintenance recommendations—preventing unexpected downtime. This reduces maintenance costs by 25% while extending equipment life. You'll know exactly when to service equipment before problems occur.

Can I control batching remotely?

Yes. BatchLogic includes mobile apps for iOS and Android plus cloud-based dashboards accessible from any device. Plant managers can monitor batching in real-time, view analytics, adjust settings, and receive alerts remotely. The system works offline and automatically syncs when connectivity returns.

How is my data secured?

Enterprise-grade security includes data encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance and conduct annual security audits. Data is hosted on secure AWS servers with automatic backups every 6 hours, 99.9% uptime SLA, and disaster recovery protocols. You own all your data and can export it anytime.
