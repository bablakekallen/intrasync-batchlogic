  • ACI Certifications: Track concrete field testing technician certifications and renewal dates

  • Hazard Communication: Chemical safety training for admixtures and hazardous materials

24/7 Shift Scheduling for Continuous Operations

  • Multi-Shift Management: Day/swing/night shift scheduling with automatic shift differentials
  • Rotation Scheduling: Manage rotating shift patterns and ensure adequate coverage
  • On-Call Scheduling: Track driver and operator on-call availability for emergency deliveries
  • Skill-Based Assignments: Match certified operators to equipment and plant requirements

Certified Payroll for Government Jobs

  • WH-347 Form Generation: Automated certified payroll reporting for government contracts
  • Prevailing Wage Rates: Automatic rate tables by county and job classification
