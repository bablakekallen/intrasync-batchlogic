The Batch-to-Cash Flow

See how BatchLogic connects your plant operations directly to your financial systems with zero manual intervention.

STEP 1

Batch Ticket Created

Every load creates a ticket with exact material quantities, customer info, and delivery details.

STEP 2

Invoice Generated

Tickets automatically convert to invoices based on customer pricing agreements and terms.

STEP 3

COGS Posted

Material costs from actual batch weights flow directly to cost of goods sold.

STEP 4

Payment Collected

Electronic payment processing, cash application, and collections all in one system.

Built for Concrete Producers

Generic accounting software doesn't understand your business. BatchLogic Accounting is purpose-built for the unique financial requirements of readymix and concrete operations.

Ticket-Based Billing

  • Customer pricing: Manage complex pricing matrices with volume discounts

Profitability Analytics
