About IntraSync Industrial

Transforming Batch Plant Operations Through Innovation

At IntraSync Industrial, we're revolutionizing how concrete producers operate through cutting-edge batch control technology and industry expertise. Our mission is straightforward yet ambitious: to unlock your batch plant's true potential with AI-powered automation, proprietary sensors, and data-driven optimization tools.

Leadership That Delivers Results

Zachary Frye - Chief Technology Officer & Founder

Zachary Frye founded IntraSync Industrial with a vision to revolutionize the batch plant and concrete manufacturing industry through innovative control systems and AI-powered automation. With a foundation in Mechanical Engineering from California State University-Sacramento and specialized expertise in Graphic Design from Sierra College, Zach brings a unique blend of technical depth and design thinking to industrial automation and control systems.

Engineering Excellence in Action

Over the past seven years, Zach has led the development and refinement of the IntraSync BatchLogic platform, pioneering advanced AI and hardware solutions that have set new industry benchmarks in batching accuracy and automation. His hands-on approach to solving real-world batch plant production challenges has resulted in:

  • Unmatched Accuracy: Clients achieve 99.9% batch accuracy—10x better than standard controllers through proprietary sensor technology.
  • Industry-Leading Hardware: Development of custom-engineered load cells, control units, and sensor systems designed specifically for concrete batching applications.
  • AI Innovation: Advanced machine learning algorithms for autonomous batch optimization, predictive maintenance, and self-learning systems that improve continuously.
  • Operational Excellence: Delivered measurable improvements including 30% waste reduction, 15% throughput increase, and 45% downtime reduction through intelligent automation.

Technology Leadership & Innovation

Under Zach's technical leadership, IntraSync has built a comprehensive batch control platform featuring:

  • Proprietary Hardware: Custom-designed IntraSync Load Cells (±0.1% accuracy) and industrial-grade Control Units with real-time processing.
  • Universal Integration: Seamless connectivity with all major batch plant equipment—Command, ConTech, Astec, Liebherr, Hess, Elkon, Olin, and more.
  • AI-Powered Capabilities: Advanced Machine Learning implementation for batch optimization, predictive maintenance, anomaly detection, and autonomous control.
  • Team Development: Successfully leads a skilled engineering team to create sophisticated systems that enhance production efficiency, accuracy, and profitability across concrete manufacturing.
