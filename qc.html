BatchLogic QC Features

Precision quality control for every batch with real-time monitoring and automated validation

98% Batch Accuracy

Precision software algorithms deliver industry-leading accuracy with your existing load cells and sensors. Real-time monitoring of every material with automatic drift compensation and calibration alerts.

Moisture Compensation

Real-time moisture sensing with automatic water adjustment. Receives aggregate moisture data from MaterialsLogic for even more precise compensation.

Temperature Monitoring

Multi-zone temperature tracking with automatic ice/heat adjustments. Ensure consistent concrete temperature year-round for optimal strength development.

Slump Prediction

AI-powered slump prediction based on aggregate properties, mix design, and environmental conditions. Validate target slump before the truck leaves.

Admixture Accuracy

Precision chemical dispensing with flow meters and peristaltic pumps. Multi-admixture support with sequencing control and dosage verification.

Mix Validation

Automated mix design validation before batching. Verify material certifications, check tolerances, and ensure compliance with project specifications.

Real-Time QC Alerts

Instant notifications when batches deviate from specifications. Alerts to operators, QC managers, and dispatch with configurable thresholds.

Batch Tickets & Records

Complete batch documentation with material weights, temperatures, times, and QC status. Digital tickets flow to dispatch and CastLogic Verify.

CastLogic ERP Module

CastLogic Verify

Your batch data becomes even more powerful when combined with CastLogic's comprehensive precast quality management

AI Computer Vision Inspections

Automated defect detection on finished precast pieces. Link surface defects back to batch parameters—temperature, slump, mix design—for root cause analysis.

Defect Tracking & NCRs

Non-conformance logging with photo documentation. Root cause analysis traces defects back to specific batch tickets for corrective action.

PCI Compliance Management

Built-in PCI certification templates. Batch records, material certifications, and inspection results combine for complete audit documentation.

Strength Test Correlation

7-day, 14-day, and 28-day cylinder tracking linked to batch data. Correlate strength results with temperature, slump, and mix parameters for continuous improvement.

Digital QC Checklists

Custom inspection templates with conditional logic. Mobile app for field use with batch data auto-populated from BatchLogic records.

Complete Audit Trail

Full traceability from batch ticket through production to delivered precast piece. Every material, every measurement, every inspection—documented and linked.
