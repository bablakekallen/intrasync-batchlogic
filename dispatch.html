Built for Readymix Concrete Operations

Concrete delivery has unique challenges that generic dispatch systems can't handle. BatchLogic is purpose-built for the readymix industry.

Mixer Truck Management: Track mixer truck capacity (6yd, 8yd, 10yd), drum condition, maintenance schedules, and DOT compliance for your entire fleet.
Drum Revolution Tracking: Monitor drum rotations in real-time to ensure concrete quality and prevent over-rotation that could affect workability.
Slump Monitoring & Adjustments: Record slump tests, track water additions at jobsite, and maintain audit trail for specification compliance.
Concrete Delivery Timing: Optimize batch-to-pour timing based on drive time, set time, traffic conditions, and ambient temperature to ensure workability.
Pour Coordination: Manage multi-truck continuous pours with precise timing to maintain cold joints and contractor productivity requirements.
Pump Truck Scheduling: Coordinate concrete pump availability and timing with mixer deliveries for seamless placement operations.
