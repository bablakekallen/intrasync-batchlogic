How BatchLogic AI Works

1

Data Collection & Analysis

BatchLogic continuously collects high-frequency sensor data from load cells, temperature probes, moisture sensors, and equipment controllers. Every batch generates thousands of data points analyzed in real-time.

  • 1000 Hz sampling rate from all sensors
  • Pattern recognition across 10+ dimensions
  • Historical batch comparison and trending
2

Machine Learning Training

Advanced ML algorithms are trained on your plant's historical data. Models learn the relationship between ingredients, conditions, and final product characteristics. Training improves continuously as new batches are produced.

  • Deep neural networks for complex pattern recognition
  • Random forest models for decision making
  • Continuous model refinement with each batch
3

Autonomous Decision Making

AI makes real-time decisions about batch adjustments without human intervention. Before each batch starts, AI predicts the optimal ingredient proportions and processing parameters. During batching, continuous adjustments maintain perfect specifications.

  • Sub-second decision latency
  • Multi-objective optimization (quality, cost, speed)
  • Constraint satisfaction and safety bounds
4

Continuous Learning & Improvement

AI learns from actual outcomes to improve future predictions. When batches are tested and results are known, AI uses this feedback to refine its models. System performance improves exponentially over time.

  • Reinforcement learning from test results
  • Weekly model retraining with new batch data
  • Exponential accuracy improvement curve
